About

At the age of 16, I got lucky to meet a photographer, in our over populated area, who opened my eyes to the beauty around me. This encounter altered the path of my hate towards photos in was I couldn`t imagine


Afer learning from the available matterials I had, I begarn my Photography journy through the help of my friends and family.
